## The pitch flow

**1. Open with their pain (30 sec).**
> "Right now: marks on paper, report cards take weeks, parents call your office all day asking about fees and results, and at year-end deliberation is a marathon. EDIFIS does all of it — and it speaks your parents' language."

**2. "This is YOUR school, online." (1 min)** — show **pssnkwen.myedifis.com**.
> "Every school gets its own branded site and app. Your name, your colours."

**3. Setup is yours to control (2 min)** — admin panel → show **Classes, Subjects (with coefficients), Fees per class, School type Day/Boarding/Both, Language EN/FR**.
> "You set your subjects and **coefficients**, your fee structure, your calendar — no programmer needed. Maths coefficient 4, English 3, exactly like your report cards."

**4. Teachers work in Excel — zero training (2 min)** — app as Teacher → **Excel mark sheet** → download → fill → upload.
> "Teachers download a sheet, type marks like they always have, upload. The system does the averages, grades, and ranking."

**5. The report card that's actually correct (2 min)** — Principal → **Compute** → open a **report card** → **Download PDF**.
> "Instant, coefficient-weighted, with **mention, conduct, class average, rank** — and an **AI comment per student**. Weeks of work, done in seconds. In English or French."

**6. The wow — AI (2 min).**
- **Principal:** VACUUM → ask *"Which students owe the most fees?"* / *"Who is struggling?"* → instant answer.
  > "Ask your school anything, in plain language."
- **Parent:** Ask EDIFIS AI → *"Why do I owe fees?"* → scoped answer about **their** child only.
  > "Parents get instant answers — your office phone stops ringing."

**7. Parents stay informed (1 min)** — show a **push/SMS** notification + **Fee Statement** + **report card** on the parent app.
> "Fee posted, results published — parents get notified automatically. Even basic phones, by SMS."

**8. Year-end in one click (1 min)** — Principal → **Deliberate** a class → Promoted / Conditional / Repeat, override with a reason.
> "Deliberation done in minutes, with a record of every decision."

## Handle the 3 objections
- **"Is it expensive?"** → "Less than what you lose in time and uncollected fees. We bill per school, per year — I'll show you a plan that fits your size."
- **"My teachers aren't tech people."** → "They use Excel. That's the whole point — no new skill."
- **"What about internet?"** → "Marks are done in Excel offline; you upload when connected. Parents get SMS even on basic phones."

## The close
> "Let's run **your** school on it for one term — I'll set up your classes, subjects, and fees this week. You'll have your first computer-generated report cards before mid-term. Shall we start?"
