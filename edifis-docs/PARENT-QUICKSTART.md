# EDIFIS — Parent Quickstart Guide

> **One-page guide for guardians.** See your child's results, fees, and attendance from your phone. You cannot see other students' data.

---

## Step 1 — Log in for the first time

1. Open your browser and go to **`https://pssnkwen.edifis.cm/parent`** (the school will give you the exact address).
2. Enter your **phone number** as your username.
3. For your first password, type your **phone number backwards**.
   - Example: if your phone is `670123456`, type `654321076`.
4. The system will ask you to **set a 4–6 digit PIN** — pick something you'll remember.
5. If you registered an email address with the school, you'll also get a **6-digit code** by email. Enter it to verify your device.

> **Forgot your PIN?** Contact the school secretary. They can reset your account.

---

## Step 2 — Install the app (optional but recommended)

### On your phone (Android):
1. Open **`https://pssnkwen.edifis.cm/parent`** in Chrome.
2. Tap the ⋮ menu → **"Add to Home screen"**.
3. Name it "EDIFIS" and tap Add.
4. The app icon appears on your home screen. Tap it to open.

### Enable notifications:
When prompted, allow notifications. You'll get alerts when:
- Your child's results are published
- A new fee is posted
- Your child is marked absent
- An exeat is issued
- A school event is added to the calendar

---

## What you can see

| Screen | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **My Children** | A list of all your children enrolled at the school |
| **Balance** | How much you owe in fees (total of all charges minus credits) |
| **Results** | Your child's published marks and term average |
| **Attendance** | How many sessions your child attended |
| **Calendar** | School events, holidays, exam dates |

Tap a child's name to see their individual balance, results, and attendance.

---

## Log in again

1. Open the app or go to **`https://pssnkwen.edifis.cm/parent`**.
2. Enter your **phone number** and your **PIN**.
3. If you're on a new phone, you'll get an email code — enter it to trust the new device.

---

## Quick answers

**Q: I can't see my child's results.**
A: Results only appear after the teacher publishes them. If the term is still in progress, results won't be visible yet.

**Q: The balance looks wrong.**
A: The balance is calculated automatically from all issued items and fees minus any credits or returns. If something seems off, contact the bursar.

**Q: I got a notification but can't find it.**
A: Open the app and tap the 🔔 Notices icon in the top-right. All notifications are stored there.

**Q: My phone number changed.**
A: Tell the school secretary. They will update your number in the system.

**Q: Can I see my child's results from last term?**
A: Yes — select the term/sequence from the Results screen.

**Q: Does this use my mobile data?**
A: Yes, the parent portal is on the cloud (internet). It does NOT work without an internet connection. For offline access, ask the school for a printed report card.
